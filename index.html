Cybersecurity

  • By David Aarhus, Lucas Torti, Christopher Chang, Gabriel Garcia

Topics

History

Cyber security is a relatively new field but continues to become increasingly crucial. Past events show how devastating well coordinated cyber attacks can be.

Passwords

We all forget our passwords, and they're a major pain to keep safe and unique. In addition to showing an easy technique to create a lengthy, strong password, this lesson details a secure and convenient solution: password managers.

Avoiding Malware

There are many ways hackers try to gain access to both your computer and personal information, but there are steps you can take to reduce this risk. Learn about these various forms of malware and social engineering as well as tips to avoid them in order to stay safe online.

Careers

Once you have been made aware of all the dangers of Cybersecurity, you may be interested in pursuing it as a career. Luckily, Cybersecurity positions are very sought after since it is a growing industry.
